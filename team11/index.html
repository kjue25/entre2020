About our Product

Linked keychains are a great way to decorate and personalize your backpack or purse, it's light and easy to place on your bag, comes in a few of our favorite hand picked designs, and also some of our signature clay charms! Some people might worry about the weight of our keychain, and especially so with the mention of a clay charm. But really the charm is practically weightless! The feather keychain provides a soft feel and adds both gentle and bright colors to the product! Together the feather keychain and the clay charm combine together to make the perfect add on to your bag!
