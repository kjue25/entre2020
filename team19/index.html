What is Pinned?

Pinned products are a unique way of combining art with 3D shapes and symbols. With our string art you can decorate any space neatly, and easily. Pinned products are both customizable in color and design, letting the buyer have a more personal experience. Along with every string art piece you buy, we will provide you with an inspirational message from famous idols. The messages are on colorful tags that will come with any decorative piece bought. Our products combine creativity and kindness, which we, as a company, believe is of utmost importance.

Where did our company name and logo come from?

We decided that we wanted our company name and logo to incorporate the different elements of our product: string, nails, and wood. The name Pinned was to represent the nails pinning down the string. Our logo continued off of the theme of the string and the nails. The blue shows the string spun around the nails to form letters.
