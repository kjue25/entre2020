Pack-A-Mask:

Welcome to Pack-A-Mask!
Every time you take off your mask, or bring it around with you, you bring unnecessary risks to your health and mask.
Instead of wasting the precious filters(N95 or otherwise), which are expensive and hard to come by, you can preserve
the lifespan of your mask’s filter. Pack-A-Mask is a double-layered bag of fabric, that is an additional layer of
protection, to keep away germs and bacteria from your mask. This washable and reusable mask bag has a button to keep
your mask safe, all the while keeping a slight pocket of air at the top. This prevents germs and bacteria from
growing within a humid and damp environment. Finally, this bag also has a metal clasp to attach to your jeans, or bag
for easy portability. You won’t have to worry about looks either. This bag has patterned fabric, and bright colors,
that will make sure you won’t lose the bag, and will catch the attention of others.
