Blazed

selling candles and candle holders

candles

The candles come in 5 different scents, rose, jasmine, chamomile, lavender, and peppermint. We make a conscious effort to make the candles have a natural feel, to make you believe that you are outdoors. The containers for the candles have patterns of mandalas which are normally a symbol of peace. The containers can also come in simpler designs with fewer bright colors.


candle holders

The candle holders that we have at the moment are made with natural materials of rocks and shells. The rock candle holders are made with little holes in between the rocks to let out the light of the candle. The shell candle holder is made in a way where the light is cupped in, but it still shines from the top beautifully. The bottom of the candle holders is a recycled jar lid that is either painted or covered in something such as washi tape.
