Our Story

Our names are Emma, Bhavya, and Briana and our company is Blazed. Our company sells candles and candle holders. We all love to have a nice relaxing day or afternoon and what is better than to have a nice relaxing candle. We all love to work in a helpful and healthy work enviroment and we welcome ideas and people with open arms. We make all our products in the comfort of our homes and make them with good quality clean products. If we are to get invested in, we would use better materials for both the candle and the holders. For the candle we would use soy wax because it has less of a scent and we would get better containers that have more pastel and less bright colors and patterns.

Process

We made our candles by melting the beeswax in a small pitcher over simering water. When the wax melted and then we added a couple drops of one of our essential oils. Next, we placed the wick with a sticker at the bottom of the tin well using a metal holder to keep it in place. Then we poured the melted wax into the holder and let it sit for about 20 minutes.


The process of making the candle holders was simple, easy, and fun! First, we went to our backyard and collected some nice rocks that had a variety of shapes and colors. Next, we collected a jar lid to keep as the base for the candle. This would be an adequate base because if any wax happens to spill out of the candle that is put inside, it would all be held in and not flow through the spaces in between the rocks that let out light. Next, we heated up a hot glue gun and started to piece the rocks together from the inside of the lid, purposely creating spaces. Finally, we let everything dry and then painted the lid iridescent white to make it look more professional. For the shell candle holder, we got some shells and a jar lid to start. This time, we flipped the jar lid over and started attaching shells to the top. The big ones went on the bottom and then we kept adding smaller ones on top and in the spaces to create a strong base and pretty flower. Finally, we put some nice washi tape on the lid to make it look prettier. Something you can add as your own touch to this product is some niece filler to put the candle on top of, such as beads or sand.
